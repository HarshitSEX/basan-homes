Safety Switches & Switchboards

Your Family's Safety
Comes First

Safety switches save lives. They detect electrical faults and cut power in as little as 0.03 seconds — fast enough to prevent electrocution. In Victoria, safety switches are now required on all circuits in rental properties, and strongly recommended for all homes. If your home doesn't have them on every circuit, it should.

Many older Melbourne West homes also have outdated switchboards with ceramic fuses or old circuit breakers that are no longer safe or adequate for modern electrical loads. An upgraded switchboard gives you more capacity, better safety and peace of mind.

  • Full switchboard upgrades and replacements
