What We Provide

Everything Your Agency
Needs in One Partner

From urgent tenant repair requests to major renovation projects between tenancies — Basan Homes handles it all for your agency under one agreement.

⚡

Urgent Repairs

We respond to urgent tenant repair requests within 24 hours — often same day. Burst pipes, no hot water, broken locks, electrical faults — we treat urgent jobs as urgent and keep both your landlord and tenant informed throughout.

🔄

Between-Tenancy Make-Ready

When a tenant vacates, we turn properties around fast — patching walls, repainting, cleaning, fixing damaged items, replacing broken fixtures and getting the property back to market condition as quickly as possible.

Property Condition Reports

Detailed written condition reports with timestamped photos for ingoing and outgoing inspections, periodic inspection follow-ups and maintenance assessment reports. PDF delivered within 48 hours.

Renovation Projects

When your landlord clients want to renovate to increase rental yield or sale price, we handle the full project — kitchen renovations, bathroom updates, fresh paint throughout, new flooring and more.

Maintenance Reporting

After every job we provide a written report with before and after photos, work completed, materials used and any items identified for future attention. Your landlords and PM team stay fully informed.
