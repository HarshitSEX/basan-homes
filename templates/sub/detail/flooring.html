Damaged flooring is more than just an eyesore. Loose tiles are a trip hazard. Squeaky floorboards disrupt sleep. Damaged vinyl lifts at the edges and continues to deteriorate. Left unaddressed, small flooring problems always become bigger and more expensive ones.

Our flooring team repairs all types of residential and commercial flooring across Melbourne's West. We carry common materials and adhesives on our vehicles and match existing floors as closely as possible, so repairs blend in rather than stand out.

  • Squeaky and bouncy floorboard repairs
  • Timber board replacement and colour matching
  • Loose, cracked and chipped tile replacement
  • Re-grouting and grout colour matching
  • Hybrid and vinyl plank repairs
  • Subfloor repairs and levelling compounds
  • Carpet re-stretching and seam repairs
  • Floor sanding, staining and polishing
  • New flooring supply and installation
