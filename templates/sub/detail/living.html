Living Area Renovations

Transform How
Your Family Lives

Many Melbourne West homes were built with separate, closed-off rooms that feel dark and disconnected. Removing walls to create an open-plan kitchen, dining and living space is one of the most impactful renovations you can do, and one of the best for resale value.

Before any wall comes down, we assess the structure properly. Some walls are load-bearing and require a structural engineer's approval and a steel beam installation. We manage that entire process, engineer, permits, steel, plastering and painting, so the finished result looks like it was always that way.

Beyond wall removal, we also renovate living areas with new flooring, fresh paint, updated lighting, built-in storage and feature wall installations that completely refresh how a room feels.

What We Cover

Our Living Area Renovation Services

Wall Removal

Safe, clean wall removal including structural assessment, engineer approval where required, steel beam installation, plasterboard patching, cornice repair and a paint-ready finish throughout.

New Flooring

Hybrid plank, engineered timber, polished concrete, tiles and carpet, supplied and installed throughout living areas. We remove existing flooring, prepare the subfloor and install to a high standard.

Lighting Upgrade

Recessed downlight installation, pendant light wiring, dimmer switches and LED strip lighting. Good lighting design completely transforms how a living space feels, brighter, warmer and more welcoming.

Feature Walls

Timber slat walls, shiplap cladding, stone veneer and painted feature walls that add depth and character to living spaces. We design and build feature walls that become the focal point of the room.

Built-In Storage

Custom-built entertainment units, bookshelves, window seats and storage cabinetry that maximise your living space and reduce clutter. All joinery is made to measure for a clean, integrated look.

Painting

Full interior repaint with proper surface preparation, quality paints and a clean, sharp finish. We paint ceilings, walls, doors, skirting boards and architraves, leaving every surface looking fresh and new.
