A renovated bathroom is one of the best investments you can make in your home. It adds real value at sale, improves your daily routine and transforms one of the most-used rooms in the house. Whether it's a small bathroom in an older Melbourne West home or a large ensuite in a newer build, we approach every job with the same attention to detail.

The most important part of any bathroom renovation is waterproofing, and it's exactly where many cheap operators cut corners. Every bathroom we build is fully waterproofed AS3740, inspected and certified before a single tile goes down. This protects your home from water damage for decades.

From frameless glass shower screens and wall-hung vanities to heated floors and freestanding baths, we have the skills and experience to bring any bathroom vision to life, on time and on budget.

What We Cover

Everything in Your Bathroom Renovation

️

Waterproofing

Full waterproofing of all wet areas using a two-coat membrane system, properly lapped into drains, corners and wall junctions. Inspected and certified to AS3740 before tiling begins. This is the foundation of a bathroom that lasts.

Tiling

Floor and wall tiling in porcelain, ceramic, natural stone or large-format tiles. We use precision cutting equipment and lay tiles to consistent grout lines. Our tilers take real pride in their work, the finish shows it.

Shower & Bath

Frameless and semi-frameless shower screen installation, shower base supply and installation, freestanding and built-in bath installation, shower niche construction and all associated plumbing connections by our our team.

Vanity & Toilet

Wall-hung and floor-standing vanity installation, mirror and shaving cabinet fitting, toilet suite supply and installation, and all associated plumbing. We help you select products that match your chosen style and budget.

Lighting & Electrical

Recessed downlight installation, exhaust fan supply and fitting, heated towel rail wiring, in-floor heating installation and all other electrical work. Certificates of compliance provided on every job.

Accessories & Finish

Towel rails, toilet roll holders, robe hooks, soap dispensers and all finishing accessories coordinated to match your tapware finish. Final grouting, siliconing and a thorough clean before handover.
