Full Home Renovations

Every Room,
Completely Renewed

Many homeowners renovate their home one room at a time over many years, and end up with a house that never quite feels cohesive. A full home renovation solves that problem completely. Every room is updated at once, with consistent finishes, matching materials and a unified design that flows from the front door to the back.

A full renovation also makes financial sense. Doing everything at once means tradespeople are on site once, disruption happens once and the cost per room is significantly lower than individual room renovations done separately over time.

Basan Homes manages the entire project with one dedicated project manager. You have a single point of contact throughout, someone who knows your project inside out and keeps everything on track.
