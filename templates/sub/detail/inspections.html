Know Your Property's
Electrical Condition

Buying a property is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll ever make. An electrical inspection before purchase can reveal hidden faults, outdated wiring, non-compliant installations and safety hazards that a standard building inspection might miss. Knowing about electrical issues before settlement gives you negotiating power and protects you from unexpected costs.

For landlords, electrical compliance is a legal requirement in Victoria. Our inspections check that your rental property meets current safety standards and provide the documentation you need to demonstrate compliance to your property manager and tenants.

  • Pre-purchase electrical safety inspections
  • Rental property compliance inspections
  • Safety switch presence and function testing
  • Switchboard and wiring condition assessment
  • Smoke alarm compliance checking
  • Identification of non-compliant installations
  • Written inspection report with photos
  • Remediation recommendations and quotes
