Kitchen Renovations

The Heart of
Every Home

The kitchen is where families gather every single day, for breakfast before school, dinner after work, weekend cooking and everything in between. A well-designed kitchen makes all of that better. More storage, better workflow, more natural light, surfaces that are easy to clean and a space that genuinely makes you happy to walk into.

At Basan Homes, we've renovated kitchens of every size and style across Melbourne's West, from compact single-wall kitchens in older homes to large island-bench designs in new builds. We understand Australian kitchens, Australian families and Australian cooking habits.

We manage the entire process, cabinetmakers, stonemasons, plumbers, electricians and tilers, so you have one contact, one quote and one team responsible for the result from start to finish.

What We Cover

Every Part of Your Kitchen Renovation

📐

Design & Layout

We start by understanding how you actually use your kitchen. Where do you cook? How do you store things? Do you entertain? From there we design a layout that genuinely works for your household, whether that's a galley, L-shape, U-shape or island configuration.

🪵

Cabinetry

Cabinets are the foundation of any kitchen renovation. We work with quality cabinet suppliers to deliver doors, drawers and storage solutions that fit your space exactly, in a wide range of finishes from matte white and timber veneer to two-pac polyurethane and handleless profiles.

🪨

Benchtops

Your benchtop is the most-used surface in your kitchen. We supply and install engineered stone, natural stone, laminate and timber benchtops, including waterfall edges, undermount sink cutouts and breakfast bar overhangs. Every benchtop is templated on-site for a perfect fit.

💧

Plumbing

Our our team handle all sink installations, tapware fitting, dishwasher connections and any plumbing relocations required by your new kitchen layout. We use quality fittings and make sure everything is properly tested before handover.

⚡

Electrical

Rangehood wiring, under-cabinet LED lighting, power point upgrades, USB outlets and appliance circuit installation, all carried out by our our team with quality work provided.

🧱

Splashbacks & Tiling

The splashback ties your kitchen's look together. We install glass, porcelain tile, stone and stainless steel splashbacks, cut to fit precisely around power points and windows. Our tilers work to a high standard with consistent grout lines and clean, sharp edges.
